SCOPE OF DELETION REQUEST:
I request deletion of ALL personal data you hold about me. "Personal data" under the CPA means information that is linked or reasonably linkable to an identified or identifiable individual, excluding de-identified data and publicly available information.

This includes but is not limited to:
  - Identifiers and contact information
  - Commercial and transactional data
  - Internet and electronic network activity
  - Geolocation data
  - Inferences drawn to create a consumer profile
  - Sensitive data (racial/ethnic origin, religious beliefs, mental or physical health diagnosis, sex life or sexual orientation, citizenship or immigration status, genetic or biometric data, personal data of a known child, precise geolocation data)

  (b) Processing my personal data for targeted advertising; and
  (c) Profiling me in furtherance of decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects.
