Identity Theft Prevention Checklist

Complete these steps to maximize your protection against identity theft.

Financial Monitoring Setup Guide

Check your free credit reports weekly
Visit AnnualCreditReport.com — the official site for free reports from all 3 bureaus.
Set up bank transaction alerts
Enable instant notifications for any transaction above $0 on all checking, savings, and credit accounts.
Use a dedicated email for financial accounts
Separate your financial email from your general-use email to reduce phishing exposure.
Enable SIM lock with your carrier
Prevent SIM swapping by adding a PIN to your mobile carrier account (AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile).
Consider credit monitoring
Free options: Credit Karma, Experian free monitoring. Many banks also offer free credit score tracking.
